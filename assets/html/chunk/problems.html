Problem statement and solution overview:

Lack of transparency in the real estate market:

PINAGORA provides users with accurate and up-to-date information about properties for sale in the UAE, helping them make informed decisions.

Difficulty in finding the right property:

With the help of advanced search filters and algorithms, PINAGORA can match users with properties that meet their specific criteria, making the process of finding the right property much easier.

Time-consuming property viewings:

By offering virtual property tours and 3D floor plans, our app can allow users to explore properties from the comfort of their own home, saving time and effort on physical viewings.

Language barriers:

Many expatriates and international buyers in the UAE may face language barriers when dealing with local real estate agents. Our app can provide multilingual support, helping users navigate the purchasing process more effectively.

Legal complexities:

Purchasing real estate in the UAE can involve a complex legal process that may be confusing for first-time buyers. PINAGORA can provide guidance on legal requirements and connect users with trusted legal advisors to ensure a smooth transaction.
